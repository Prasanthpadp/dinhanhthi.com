## Device's info

~~~ python
print('cuda is available? ', torch.cuda.is_available())
print('device_count: ', torch.cuda.device_count())
print('current device: ', torch.cuda.current_device())
print('device name: ', torch.cuda.get_device_name(0))
~~~
